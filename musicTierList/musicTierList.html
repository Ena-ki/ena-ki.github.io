Sewerslvt

This album is the definition of "when you're happy you listen to the music, but when you're sad you listen to the lyrics" , except there is no lyrics and you're sad always.
So the backstory of this album is that the author lost their lover to suicide and made this album in commemoration. You can definitely feel the emotions sewerslvt tried to put in each track. I feel like the earth moves beneath my feet and the time passes too fast for me to do something about it when I listen to this album. It feels like being lost in a maze you know there is no exit in.
Definitely the album that made me love Drum and Bass genre. My favorite tracks are probably "dissociating" or "all the joy in life was gone once you left", but I also cried when I first listened to "blissful overdose" If you wanted to get into Drum and Bass this might be a great start.
